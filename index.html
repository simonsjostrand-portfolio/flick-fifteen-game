How to win - or lose 📓

  • 🖋️ Your goal is to score points by reaching exactly 15 in any slot using the current flick number. On success, you gain 1 point, and the slot resets to zero.
  • 🖋️ If a slot’s value goes over 15, it resets to zero, you lose 1 point (if you have any), and you lose 1 heart.
  • 🖋️ Click the “Flick” button below a slot to add the current flick number to it. Flick numbers range from 1 to 5. You start with 100 flicks total.
  • 🖋️ You have 3 hearts. If you lose all your hearts, it's game over.
  • 🖋️ You win by reaching 20 points before you run out of flicks or hearts.
  • 🖋️ Your highscore depends on how few flicks you used to win the game.

Good luck! 🕹️
